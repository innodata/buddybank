Capstone Project

Chatbot using DialogFlow!

PGCP DSML B#7

Problem Statement:

Financial institutions must engage with their customers and members in the right way, and at the right place and time. A good integration of chatbots as part of the engagement process can provide consumers with quick and personalized interactions, using machine learning and artificial intelligence as a foundation.

Objective of this project to develop a Dialogflow based chatbot to mimic the conversations regarding opening a bank account. The chatbot must pointed in the right direction where they can find relevant information, the engagement is more likely to result in a new account opening.
